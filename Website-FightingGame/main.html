It's a caring battle...

Objective

The object of the game is to guess who, Tibble or Banach, can beat themselves up first. Here is a little background on the characters:

Tibble is tiny warrior who likes to collect large animals. Although he is of small stature, he is NOT very accurante with his punches and often fails to hit himself. However, when he lands a punch, it is a HUGE blow!

Banach is a tall sneaky rouge who likes to collect toys. He is bigger than Tibble and is incredibly accurante punching himself. BUT, he is not very strong so he can't hit himself very hard.

Who will beat themselves up first?

Background

It is not clear why Tibble and Banach beat themselves up in this game - but they do. They are both caring individuals who enjoy delivering high damage. Their caring nature is so big, that it often overpowers them. This could be the reason why they choose to test their abilities by beating themselves up instead.
